  • Wallet ActivationOpen or Close

    Activate your UBM wallet in 3 simple steps

    Step-1 - Type 'ACTIVATE' and send the SMS on 96860 55555

    Step-2 - Receive and answer the IVR call; set mPin and confirm the mPin

    Enter your Date of Birth (DoB) in DD/MM/YYY Y

    and mother's DoB

    Step-3 - The wallet will get activated after successful completion

    of the call

    ATM Card Activation

    Type 'ACTCARD' and send the SMS on 96860 55555

    First Call

    You can also activate your UBM wallet and ATM card by calling on

    022-3985 2616

    Note: Union Bank of India takes minimum 5 working days to verify and activate a UBM wallet
  • Wallet Cash-InOpen or Close

    OR From your nearby authorized UBM Agent

  • Manage mPINOpen or Close

    In case you forget the mPIN please follow these steps.

    Call Customer Care 1860 3000 4500 and raise your request for setting the mPIN

    After the account verification, Customer Care team would reset the new mPIN

    Receive the SMS with 4 digit temporary PIN (Valid for 24 hours)

    Type 'RESET' and send the SMS on 96860 555 55

    Receive an IVR call and select option to reset the mPIN

    Enter temporary pin (Received through SMS), create and then confirm the new mPIN
