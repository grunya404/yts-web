About Union Bank Money

Union Bank Money (UBM) offers a bouquet of mobile money services through a secure digital wallet. Avail it on your mobile phone, or walk in to the nearest YTS point.

  • Convenience

    • Make payments 24x7 anytime, anywhere
    • Access a wide network of UBM Agents, spread across India
    • Avail services through SMS, IVR or mobile application

  • Device and network independent

    • Neutral to telecom network and types of mobile handsets

  • Organizing and scheduling transactions

    • Organize and schedule financial transactions
    • Have greater control over your expenses

  • Real time

    • Payments are processed instantly

  • Secure

    • Protected with two - factor authentication

Unique Features of Union Bank Money

  • With UBM wallet you get four options to transfer money :
    • UBM to UBM Wallet : Transfer money to another UBM customer. All you require is recipient's mobile number. It's the cheapest option to transfer money.
    • UBM to any Bank Instantly : Transfer money instantly to any bank account 24x7. It's an emerging way of transferring money.
    • UBM to any Bank in a day : Transfer money to any bank account in a day. It's the most popular and reliable way of sending money.
    • UBM to any mobile number : Send Instant cash without a need of bank account or a digital wallet. All you require is recipient's mobile number.
  • Transactions as low as Rs. 10/- possible
  • Unique feature of ATM card and cash withdrawal
  • Self-assisted and agent-assisted option available for using services
