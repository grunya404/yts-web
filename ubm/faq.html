Frequently asked Questions

  • Frequently asked Questions
  •   How do I register for Union Bank Money?Open or Close

    Download, print and fill the customer registration form and carry it along with the required KYC documentation to the nearest UBM agent to get you UBM wallet. To know your nearest agent point, please call our customer care.

  •   Do these services work on any network provider?Open or Close

    The services of Union Bank Money are independent of the service provider being used and work on all service providers.

  •  Is a smart phone required for using the services of UBM?Open or Close

    The services of Union Bank Money are independent of the mobile phone being used and work on all types of mobile phones.

  •   How do I load money into my account?Open or Close

    Go to the nearest agent and the agent would load money into your account. You would soon be able to load money online also.

  •   How do I setup MPIN for the first time?Open or Close

    You can setup your MPIN at the time of Activation of your account. Once the account is created by the Agent, you have to send SMS 'ACTIVATE' to 9686055555. You will receive an IVR call verifying your date of birth and then be prompt to create and reconfirm MPIN and a mother's DOB.

  •   What if any transaction fails? Will the amount transacted be lost? Open or Close

    No, the money is not lost in a failed transaction. If the transaction fails and account balance is not updated, please allow 48 hrs for the account balance to receive funds credit again.

  •   How is my money protected? Open or Close

    Your money is in your account with the Union Bank of India bank. It's protected through MPIN, IVR verification and SMS receipts / notifications.

  •   What happens if I buy a new mobile or misplace my mobile or change my phone number? Open or Close

    Your account is associated with your mobile number and not with mobile device or your SIM card. If you want to change your mobile number you have to fill a modification form with KYC document and submit to a UBM Agent. Your account will be activated with new number within 5 working days post successful verification.

  •   What should I do if I forget my mPIN? Open or Close

    Please contact our customer care, where your mPIN will be reset once you successfully verify yourself as the genuine owner of the wallet.

  •   I have lost my ATM card. Is my account protected? Do I need to get a new ATM card? Open or Close

    Your account is safe till your mPIN has not been compromised. Please call our customer care, get your card hot-listed and get a new ATM card from a UBM Agent.

  •   What should I do if my ATM card is stuck in the machine? Open or Close

    Please note the bank name and location of the ATM, call our customer care and inform about the incidence.

  •   What should I do if my mPIN is not being recognized by an ATM? Open or Close

    Please retry (max. 2 attempts), if problem persists then call our customer care where your mPIN will be reset once your verification is done.
