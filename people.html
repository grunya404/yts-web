Life at YTS


As a young company we are enthusiastic, ambitious and innovative is our approach to business. Core to our culture is the spirit of entrepreneurship and endeavor to bring together like-minded individuals to join us, contribute and grow the professional intellect.

YTS offer a well-defined career path with ample opportunities to think, explore, learn and grow. YTS is the place to enjoy the flexibility of moving across various business functions. We love smart people with a can-do attitude

We lay strong emphasis on integrity, discipline, teamwork and performance. In our work place our teams reflect our values, our belief and our mission.

We have an interesting mix of professionals from diverse background from banking, insurance, technology and retail to bring together knowledge and experiences to the workplace, helping our businesses to grow.
