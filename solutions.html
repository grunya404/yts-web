> Financial Inclusion

YTS Wallet Solutions

YTS offers a mobile prepaid payments platform for bank and non-bank companies to deliver prepaid mobile financial services to the underserved bank customers. YTS acts as a program manager for bank to manage 'end-to-end' operations in the value chain enabling mobile money to be available for the micro-customers in cost effective manner in their neighborhood.
